As you watch the video, move the highlighted text along by pressing f (and back with d).

Press e just before the speaker says the highlighted word. You probably only need to sync the beginning of each sentence (though if you want to do more, go ahead).

Here are all the keyboard shortcuts:

Video:
  i - pause/play
  j - back
  k - forward

Text:
  f - forward (+shift to go forward a lot)
  d - backward (+shift to go back a lot)
  e - sync word with video
  c - clear word sync
